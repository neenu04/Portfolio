Platewise

A Recipe Nutrition Calculator app designed for restaurant owners and chefs. It helps users calculate and view nutritional values like calories, protein, and fats, explore recipes, and manage orders in a simple and user-friendly way.

The target users are restaurant owners, chefs, and small food businesses who need an efficient way to provide accurate nutrition information.

Role UI/UX Designer

Project Duration 4 Weeks

Read Time 5 min

View Prototype View Research Study

01. Discovery (Persona, Goals, Frustrations)

Restaurant owners and chefs often struggle to calculate accurate nutrition for their dishes.

Most available tools are designed for personal use, not restaurant workflows.

I conducted usability research with chefs and restaurant owners to understand:

  • How they currently calculate nutrition
  • What challenges they face
  • How they use digital tools in their workflow

Key insight: They need a faster way to input recipes and instantly see nutrition breakdowns without slowing down kitchen operations.

02. Define (User Journey)

Based on the research findings, several key challenges were identified:

  • Time-consuming process to manually calculate nutrition
  • Lack of tools designed for restaurant workflows
  • Difficulty navigating between recipes and data
  • Complex interfaces reducing efficiency

Problem: Restaurant professionals need a simple system to quickly calculate nutrition without extra workload.

03. Ideate (Wireframes)

I explored multiple solutions focused on simplifying user experience.

Key ideas included:

  • Step-by-step recipe input
  • Instant nutrition calculation
  • Category-based navigation
  • Real-time portion updates

The goal was to create a fast and intuitive system for restaurant use.

04. UI Design (Logo + Final UI)

The interface was designed with a clean and minimal approach for clarity.

Visual hierarchy highlights key nutrition data like calories and protein.

Typography and spacing were used to reduce cognitive load.

05. Validate (Testing Results – add when you include visuals)

Usability testing was conducted with resturanats owners and chefs to evaluate the efficiency and ease of use of the Platewise app.

Key metrics included time on task, success rate, and SUS feedback.

Results:

  • Improved efficiency
  • Reduced errors
  • Higher user satisfaction
  • Clearer structure

Conclusion
The testing results that Platewise is intuitive ,efficient and valuable for resturant professionals.The app helps them save time and provide accurate nutrition information with ease.
